FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical wash take? +
A Basic wash takes 30–45 minutes. Standard is 45–60 minutes. Premium can take 60–90 minutes. Exact time depends on vehicle size and add-ons selected.
Do I need to provide water? +
No! Our workers carry their own equipment, including water tanks, pressure washers, and cleaning supplies. You don't need to provide anything.
Is it safe for my car's paint? +
Absolutely. We use only premium, pH-balanced car care products that are gentle on paint and safe for all coatings including ceramic and PPF.
How far in advance should I book? +
You can book same-day (subject to availability) or up to 30 days in advance. We recommend booking at least 2 hours ahead for same-day service.
What if I'm not satisfied with the wash? +
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, we'll send someone back to re-do the wash for free. Or we'll give you a full refund — your choice.
Do you service apartment complexes? +
Yes! We regularly serve apartments, gated communities, office parks, and individual homes. Just ensure there's parking access for our worker.
